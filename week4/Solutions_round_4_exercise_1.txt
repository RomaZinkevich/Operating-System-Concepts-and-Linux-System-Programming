The following line does:
1. Prints the contents of all .txt files.
2. Removes all non-alphabetic characters and leaves pure words.
3. Sorts the words.
4. Counts occurences of each unique word.
5. Sorts the list of words numerically in ascending order.
6. Displays the last 10 lines (the 10 most frequent words).
7. Formats the output into five columns without a header.
